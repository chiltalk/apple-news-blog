MacBook Air M4: Apple's Lightest Laptop Gets Even Better

Published March 26, 2026 • MacBook Air M4 • 5 min read

Apple has refreshed the MacBook Air lineup with the M4 chip, bringing unprecedented performance to its thinnest and lightest laptop. The update also introduces a new Space Black color option and extended battery life.

⚡ M4 Chip: A Major Performance Jump

The M4 chip in the new MacBook Air delivers up to 40% faster CPU performance and 50% faster GPU performance compared to the M2 model. This makes the MacBook Air M4 capable of handling demanding tasks like 4K video editing, software development, and even gaming.

M4 Chip Specifications:

  • 10-core CPU with 4 performance cores and 6 efficiency cores
  • 10-core GPU with hardware-accelerated ray tracing and mesh shading
  • 16-core Neural Engine for AI and machine learning tasks
  • 8GB, 16GB, or 24GB unified memory options
  • 120GB/s memory bandwidth

🔋 All-Day Battery Life

Apple has managed to extend the already impressive battery life on the MacBook Air M4:

  • Up to 20 hours of battery life on 13-inch model
  • Up to 18 hours of battery life on 15-inch model
  • Improved power efficiency with M4's 3nm architecture
  • Fast charging: 50% charge in just 30 minutes with 70W adapter

🖥️ Display and Design

The MacBook Air M4 features the same stunning Liquid Retina display with:

  • 13.6-inch or 15.3-inch LED-backlit display
  • 2560 x 1600 resolution on 13-inch, 2880 x 1864 on 15-inch
  • 500 nits brightness
  • P3 wide color and True Tone technology
  • Notch design with 1080p FaceTime HD camera

🎨 New Space Black Color

For the first time, the MacBook Air is available in Space Black, in addition to Midnight, Starlight, and Silver. The Space Black finish features a dark anodized aluminum chassis that resists fingerprints.

🔌 Connectivity and Ports

  • MagSafe 3 charging port
  • 2 Thunderbolt 4 ports (USB-C)
  • 3.5mm headphone jack with high-impedance support
  • Wi-Fi 6E and Bluetooth 5.3

💰 Pricing

MacBook Air M4 pricing starts at:

  • 13-inch MacBook Air M4: Starting at $999
  • 15-inch MacBook Air M4: Starting at $1,199
  • Configurations with 16GB RAM start at $1,199 and $1,399 respectively
  • Available now at Apple.com and authorized resellers

📝 Verdict

The MacBook Air M4 is an impressive update that solidifies Apple's position in the thin-and-light laptop market. With the M4 chip, extended battery life, and the new Space Black option, it's the best MacBook Air yet. For users coming from an Intel Mac or an older M1/M2 model, the upgrade is definitely worth considering.
