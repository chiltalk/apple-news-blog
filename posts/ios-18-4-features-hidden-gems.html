Everything New in iOS 18.4: Features, Changes, and Hidden Gems

Published March 22, 2026 • iOS 18.4 • 8 min read

Apple's iOS 18.4 update brings a mix of quality-of-life improvements, new AI features, and several hidden gems that flew under the radar. While not a massive overhaul like iOS 18.0 or 18.2, this update refines the experience and adds features you'll actually use daily.

🚀 Major New Features

1. Enhanced Apple Intelligence Integration

iOS 18.4 deepens Apple's AI integration with several new capabilities:

  • Smarter Siri: Now understands context better across apps and can handle follow-up questions more naturally
  • AI-Powered Photo Editing: New "Intelligent Enhance" button in Photos app automatically improves lighting, composition, and color
  • Writing Tools: New AI-powered suggestions in Mail and Notes help you write more clearly and professionally
  • Smart Search: Spotlight now uses AI to understand natural language queries better

2. Customizable Control Center

Apple finally lets you fully customize Control Center! You can now:

  • Add third-party widgets to Control Center
  • Rearrange controls to your preference
  • Create multiple Control Center profiles for different activities (Work, Home, etc.)
  • Add quick actions for your most-used apps

Pro Tip: Create a "Gaming" Control Center profile with Game Center, Screen Recording, and Do Not Disturb.

3. Redesigned Weather App

The Weather app gets a major visual overhaul:

  • New animated backgrounds that reflect current conditions
  • Hourly forecasts now show more detail
  • Severe weather alerts are more prominent
  • New "Weather Map" view shows precipitation, wind, and temperature layers

🎨 Design & UI Changes

New Emoji and Memoji

  • 50+ new emoji including phoenix, lime, and several smileys
  • New Memoji hairstyles, accessories, and clothing options
  • Improved Memoji customization with better color matching

App Icon Redesigns

Several Apple apps received subtle icon updates:

  • App Store: Cleaner, more modern look
  • Apple Music: Slightly refreshed design
  • Podcasts: New icon that better matches the app

💎 Hidden Gems

1. Quick Notes Swipe Gesture

Swipe diagonally from bottom-right corner anywhere on screen to quickly open a new note. Works in any app!

2. Battery Percentage on All Devices

Battery percentage icon is now available on all iPhone models, not just Pro models. Go to Settings → Battery → Battery Percentage to enable.

3. Hidden Camera Grid

The Camera app now has a hidden 9x9 grid option. Go to Settings → Camera → Composition → enable "Extra Grid Lines" for professional framing assistance.

4. Text Replacement for Emojis

Create keyboard shortcuts that replace text with emoji sequences! Go to Settings → General → Keyboard → Text Replacement. Example: Type "shrug" to get ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

5. Back Tap Custom Actions

Back Tap now supports more actions including taking screenshots, launching Siri, or opening specific apps. Go to Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Back Tap to customize.

📱 Performance & Battery

Early testing shows iOS 18.4 brings solid performance improvements:

  • Battery Life: 5-8% improvement in battery drain for most users
  • App Launch Speed: 10-15% faster app launches on iPhone 14 and newer
  • Animation Smoothness: Reduced stutter in multitasking and app switching
  • Thermal Management: Better heat dissipation during intensive tasks

⚠️ Known Issues

While generally stable, iOS 18.4 has a few known issues:

  • Some users report Bluetooth connectivity issues with older accessories
  • CarPlay occasionally fails to connect on first attempt
  • Apple Watch pairing sometimes takes longer than usual

Note: Apple is aware of these issues and expects fixes in iOS 18.4.1.

✅ Should You Update?

Yes, for most users. iOS 18.4 offers a solid set of improvements and new features without major bugs. The customizable Control Center alone makes it worth updating.

However, if you rely on Bluetooth accessories extensively or use CarPlay daily, you might want to wait for 18.4.1 which will address those issues.

📝 Summary

iOS 18.4 is a solid incremental update that refines the iOS 18 experience. Key highlights:

  • ✅ Enhanced Apple Intelligence across the system
  • ✅ Fully customizable Control Center
  • ✅ Several hidden gems that improve daily use
  • ✅ Performance and battery improvements
  • ⚠️ Minor Bluetooth and CarPlay issues for some users

While not a groundbreaking update, iOS 18.4 shows Apple's commitment to refining and improving iOS with each release. The features here are genuinely useful and will make your daily iPhone experience better.
