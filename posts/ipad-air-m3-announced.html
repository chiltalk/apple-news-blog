Apple Unveils iPad Air M3: Performance Leap and New Features

Published March 27, 2026 • iPad Air M3 • 6 min read

Apple today announced the new iPad Air M3, bringing the powerful M3 chip to its popular mid-range tablet lineup. The update delivers significant performance improvements and new features that bridge the gap between the Air and Pro models.

🚀 Performance: M3 Chip Arrives

The M3 chip in the new iPad Air delivers up to 35% faster CPU performance and 40% faster GPU performance compared to the previous M2 model. This makes it the fastest iPad Air ever and capable of handling demanding tasks like video editing, 3D rendering, and gaming with ease.

Key Performance Highlights:

  • 8-core CPU with 4 performance cores and 4 efficiency cores
  • 9-core GPU with hardware-accelerated ray tracing
  • 16-core Neural Engine for on-device AI tasks
  • 8GB or 16GB unified memory options
  • 100GB/s memory bandwidth

🎨 Display Improvements

While maintaining the same 11-inch and 13-inch screen sizes, the new iPad Air M3 features an upgraded Liquid Retina display with improved brightness and color accuracy:

  • 600 nits peak brightness (up from 500 nits)
  • P3 wide color gamut with full sRGB
  • ProMotion technology with 120Hz refresh rate
  • True Tone and Adaptive HDR support

📸 Camera and Connectivity

Apple has updated the camera system on the new iPad Air M3:

  • 12MP Wide rear camera with Smart HDR 4 and improved low-light performance
  • 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage support
  • Wi-Fi 6E for faster wireless connectivity
  • Bluetooth 5.3
  • 5G models now support more bands worldwide

🎵 Audio Enhancements

The new iPad Air features an upgraded speaker system with improved spatial audio support. When paired with the new Magic Keyboard (sold separately), users get a premium laptop-like experience with larger trackpad and Function row keys.

💰 Pricing and Availability

The iPad Air M3 starts at the same price as its predecessor:

  • iPad Air M3 11-inch: Starting at $599 (Wi-Fi), $749 (Wi-Fi + Cellular)
  • iPad Air M3 13-inch: Starting at $799 (Wi-Fi), $949 (Wi-Fi + Cellular)
  • Storage options: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB

Pre-orders begin today, with availability starting April 4, 2026.

📝 Our Take

The iPad Air M3 is a compelling upgrade for anyone using an older iPad model. The M3 chip brings desktop-class performance to the Air lineup, making it capable of handling professional workloads that were previously reserved for the iPad Pro. Combined with the improved display and accessories, the iPad Air M3 is an excellent choice for students, professionals, and creatives alike.
