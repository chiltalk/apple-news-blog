Apple Watch Ultra 3: New Sensors, Better Battery, and a Fresh Look

Published March 25, 2026 • Apple Watch Ultra 3 • 7 min read

Apple has announced the Apple Watch Ultra 3, the most advanced Apple Watch ever made. Building on the success of the Ultra 2, this year's model introduces new health sensors, extended battery life, and a striking new color option.

🏥 New Health Sensors

The Apple Watch Ultra 3 introduces groundbreaking health monitoring features:

Blood Glucose Monitoring

After years of development, Apple has finally brought non-invasive blood glucose monitoring to the Apple Watch Ultra 3. Using a sophisticated array of sensors and algorithms, the watch can estimate blood glucose levels without breaking the skin—revolutionizing diabetes management for millions of users.

Blood Pressure Tracking

The Ultra 3 can now track blood pressure trends using an improved optical heart rate sensor and new calibration process. Users can take readings throughout the day and view trends over time.

Enhanced Sleep Apnea Detection

Building on the sleep apnea detection introduced in watchOS 11, the Ultra 3 offers more accurate detection with additional breathing pattern analysis.

🔋 72-Hour Battery Life

Apple has made significant improvements to battery life:

  • Up to 72 hours in Low Power Mode (up from 60 hours)
  • Up to 36 hours of regular use
  • Improved efficiency with the new S9 chip
  • Fast charging: 0-80% in just 45 minutes

🖤 New Titanium Black Color

For the first time, the Apple Watch Ultra is available in a black titanium finish. This new color option features a dark DLC (Diamond-Like Carbon) coating that is both scratch-resistant and visually striking. The Alpine Loop and Trail Loop bands are also available in matching black colorways.

⚡ S9 SiP Performance

The new S9 chip in Apple Watch Ultra 3 brings:

  • 60% faster GPU performance than S8
  • New 4-core Neural Engine for on-device processing
  • Improved Siri response times
  • Double the storage at 64GB

🏔️ Durability Improvements

The Apple Watch Ultra 3 maintains its rugged design with improvements:

  • IP6X dust resistance (improved from IP6X dust resistance)
  • WR100 water resistance (100m rating)
  • EN13319 certification for recreational scuba diving to 40m
  • Temperature tolerance from -20°C to 55°C

📱 Display

The Always-On Retina display has been enhanced:

  • 3000 nits peak brightness (up from 2000 nits)
  • Better visibility in direct sunlight
  • Improved low-power mode that maintains color

💰 Pricing and Availability

  • Apple Watch Ultra 3: Starting at $799
  • Available colors: Natural Titanium, Black Titanium
  • Pre-orders begin March 28, 2026
  • Available in stores April 4, 2026

📝 Our Analysis

Apple Watch Ultra 3 represents a significant step forward in wearable health technology. The addition of blood glucose monitoring is a game-changer for diabetic users, while the extended battery life addresses one of the few complaints about previous models. The new black titanium option also makes it more versatile for everyday wear.
